COURSES AND SCHEMES


Banasthali Vidyapith's work in the field of women's education has been of a special type. It can neither be classified as routine type nor welfare type of work. It is a unique blending of the two types. In this regard the courses offered by the school education wing of the Banasthali Vidyapith are as follows :

> Elementary Education

> Secondary & Senior Secondary

Elementary Education :-

The following shall be the subject of study from class 1 to 8.

  1. Class 1 to 2 :
    1. Hindi
    2. Maths
    3. English
    4. Drawing
    5. Music
  2. Class 3 to 5 :
    1. Hindi
    2. Maths
    3. English
    4. Drawing
    5. Music
    6. Environmental Studies
    7. Cooking
    8. Introduction to Computer
    9. Craft
  3. Class 6 to 8 :
    1. Hindi
    2. Maths
    3. English
    4. Sanskrit
    5. Music/Drawing
    6. Social Studies
    7. General Science
    8. Home Science
    9. Introduction to Computer
    10. Craft

Secondary Education :-

    2. Geography
  5. General Science :

  6. Mathematics
  7. Education for Home
  8. Aesthetic Education :
    1. Music (Vocal & Instrumental)
    2. Drawing & Painting
    3. Dance : Kathak / Manipuri / Bharatnatyam
  9. Introduction to Computer
  10. Physical Education
  11. Social Services


NOTE:- New admissions are made in class 11th only.
